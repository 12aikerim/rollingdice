Instructions

In this game you both players have two strategies. You will not be able to see the other player’s choice until you submit your choice and the other player will not see your choice. You will earn points in each round according to the following rules:

In each cell the number on the left indicates points for Player A and the number on the right indicates points for Player B.

For example, upper right cell indicates {{Constants.ur|c}} for Player A and {{Constants.ru|c}} for Player B.

A) If you are player A you will move UP or DOWN.

  • If you move UP you will earn {{Constants.ul}}.
  • If you move DOWN, you will earn {{Constants.dl}} or {{Constants.dr}} depending on Player B’s move.
  • You will get {{Constants.dl}} if Player B moves LEFT
  • You will get {{Constants.dr}} if Player B moves RIGHT

B) If you are Player B you will move LEFT or RIGHT.

  • If you move LEFT you will earn {{Constants.lu}} .
  • If you move RIGHT, you will earn {{Constants.ru}} or {{Constants.rd}} depending on Player A’s move.
  • You will get {{Constants.ru}} if Player A moves UP
  • You will get {{Constants.rd}} if Player A moves DOWN.
