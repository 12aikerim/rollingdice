Instructions

In this game, you will be randomly and anonymously paired with another participant. At the beginning of each round you will randomly set a role of either Inspector or a Firm. Each of you simultaneously and privately chooses whether you want to comply/not comply if you are a Firm and inspect/not inspect if you are the Inspector. Your payoffs will be determined by the choices of both as below:

In each cell, the amount to the left is the payoff for the Inspector and to the right for the Firm.

For example, if Firm decides to Fully comply and Inspector chooses No inspection, Firm gets 100 points and Inspector - 400 points.
